Pizza

Description

Pizza, an iconic dish originating from Italy, is celebrated for its versatile and universally beloved combination of flavors. At its core, pizza features a round, flattened dough base that is traditionally topped with a layer of savory tomato sauce and a generous sprinkling of melted mozzarella cheese. This classic foundation can be adorned with a variety of toppings, including pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, and fresh basil, allowing for endless customization to suit different tastes and dietary preferences. Baked in a hot oven until the crust is crisp and the cheese is bubbly, pizza embodies a perfect harmony of textures and flavors. Its wide appeal and adaptability make it a staple in both casual dining and gourmet cuisine around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Add cheese and toppings
  5. Bake the pizza
