Burger

Description

The burger, a popular and iconic food item, typically consists of a ground meat patty, often beef, sandwiched between two halves of a bun. It is often complemented by various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. Originating from the United States, the burger has become a global culinary staple, with countless variations to suit diverse tastes and dietary preferences. Whether enjoyed at fast-food joints, gourmet restaurants, or backyard barbecues, the burger's versatility and satisfying combination of flavors and textures make it a beloved dish worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger bun
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, Mustard, Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Prepare the Patties
  2. Preheat the grill or pan
  3. Cook the patties
  4. Toast the buns
  5. Assemble the burger
