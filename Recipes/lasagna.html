Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish renowned for its rich layers of flavor and hearty ingredients. Typically, it consists of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, a savory meat sauce made from ground beef or sausage, a creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese mixture, and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
