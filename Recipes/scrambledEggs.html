Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish known for their simplicity and creamy texture. Made by whisking eggs and cooking them gently in a skillet with a bit of butter or oil, scrambled eggs are seasoned with salt and pepper to taste. The cooking process involves stirring the eggs continuously to create soft, fluffy curds. They can be enjoyed plain or enhanced with various additions such as cheese, herbs, vegetables, or meats. Versatile and quick to prepare, scrambled eggs are a nutritious and satisfying option for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Add the eggs
  4. Stir the eggs from edge to center
  5. Season and serve
